of margin per hectare

Average productivity 53 sc/ha
Break-even (total costs) 51 sc/ha
At R$110/sack — which happened in 2024 — margin turns negative.
Two sacks of margin. That is the financial context in which every input decision, every new technology, and every regenerative solution pitch gets evaluated. There is almost no room for financial risk he cannot quantify.
04 — The Twist
The farmer doesn't think
in dollars.
He doesn't think in reais.
He thinks in sacks.
Those dollar figures are how the problem looks from the outside. From inside a Brazilian farm, every decision is made in one unit only: the sack. How many sacks does this cost? How many does it save or generate? That is the only calculation that survives contact with the field.

06 — The Farmer's Filter
When a regenerative solution enters Brazil, the farmer evaluates it against three questions. Every pitch. Every time.
→
Does this reduce my fertilizer bill?
→
Does this protect my yield during transition?
→
Can I defend this to my bank in sacks per hectare?
If the answer is unclear, the pilot runs once.
And dies before the second cycle.
